Membership Level Benefits

Non-Profit (NP) Membership


  • There is no fee for non-profit organizations.
  • Belonging to the Chamber offers your business access to an established network where business can meet.
  • The Chamber is a platform for being heard, finding and maximizing opportunities..
  • Opportunities for collaboration and partnerships with other non-profit and business members.

Bronze Membership


  • Affordable membership fee.
  • All the benefits of NP Membership, plus:
  • Exclusive invitations to Chamber-sponsored events, including business luncheons, workshops, and seminars.
  • Helping you grow your business in a structured manner.
  • Assisting you to develop and manage your business processes.

Silver Membership


  • Membership fee at a higher investment level.
  • All the benefits of Bronze Membership, including:
  • Helping you to strengthen the competitiveness of your subsector and value chains.
  • Enhanced exposure with featured spotlights on the Chamber's home page and social media platforms.
  • Display of your organization's marketing materials at Chamber events and the Chamber office.

Gold Membership


  • Membership fee offering :
  • All the benefits of Silver Membership, including:
  • We also offer access to partners to unlock opportunities, through Chapter coffee clubs/networking
  • Premier positioning with top-tier logo placement on the Chamber's website and promotional materials.
  • We also offer access to partners to unlock opportunities, through Tenders
