Cape Town Chamber Of Commerce

Overview

Purpose

The Cape Town Chamber of Commerce is a chamber that help people grow their business. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, we have training session every year to help member have the idea on how to grow their business.

Scenarios

Some highlights from our launch event with Henley Business School yesterday which featured a panel discussion on "Strategy for Businesses in Cape Town," between Prof. Jon Foster-Pedley, Dean of Henley Business School, and John Lawson, CEO of the Cape Chamber of Commerce and Industry. Our first event for the year is underway with the Tygerberg Chapter hosting the First Minute Workshop with Chris Fenning at the Sanlam headquarters.
